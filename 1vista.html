1 Vista Rise, Maribyrnong

The Number One

Welcome home to 1 Vista Rise, BRAND NEW home tucked away on a quiet street in the Prestigious Edgewater Estate

Just moments from the Maribyrnong River with lush parklands, playgrounds and excellent walking tracks, there is plenty for the whole family to explore and enjoy. Conveniently situated close to excellent amenities including multiple public transport options, fantastic schools, Victoria University, and many trendy cafes and restaurants

Capturing arguably some of Edgewater's best views, this custom-designed family residence enjoys a prestigious position of Maribyrnong. The home offers quality craftsman-built interiors that are flooded with natural light and showcase top-quality finishes. Luxury features include an internal glass feature wall on the ground floor (in between garage and entry), four bedrooms, two separate living areas - one on each level, two separate bathrooms and a powder room, the master suite sports an exceptionally large robe, a walk in robe, and private courtyard. The stylish kitchen includes a butler's pantry overlooking expansive open living and dining.

Plus, a large roof deck sporting stunning city views. The ceilings will be high - 3m to the ground floor and first floor and 3.5m to the living area on the first floor.

Other features:
- uPVC framed double glazed windows
- Heating system each level
- 0.5km to Maribyrnong river
- 0.8km to Footscray Park
- 1km to Maribyrnong College
- 1400m to Highpoint Shopping Centre, VIctoria University
