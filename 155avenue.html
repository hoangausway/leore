155 The Avenue, Sunshine West

First Home Buyer and Investors Calling

Spacious living and clever design sets apart this very attractive Townhouse.
Comprising of 3 spacious bedrooms, master with ensuite, large open plan living area combining lounge room and dining adjoining stunning gourmet kitchen with stone bench tops and entrance to remote control double garage from the rear.

The open plan / living area awash with natural light, is perfect for entertaining and spills out onto private court yard for alfresco flair and enjoyment.
Location says it all. 155 The Avenue sits right on the entrance to the famous The Avenue strip of Sunshine West. Just a few short steps to the local shopping centre and opposite the Early Learning Centre,
2 minutes walk to Coles supermarket & local shops.
Only a 7 minutes walk or 2 minute drive to Sunshine West Primary school, and West Sunshine Community Centre.
Entry to Western Ring Road only a few minutes away.
Featuring:
- Built-in robes
- Split system air conditioning
- Large bathroom
- Study corner
