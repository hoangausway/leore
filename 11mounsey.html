11 Mounsey Court, Sunbury

Mounsey's Gem

For the family seeking space, flexibility and excellent connectivity to amenities, this classic home is a must see.

Sitting on the large block of land (approximately 736m2), this freshly renovated home will appeal to many astute buyers

Inside the home you will find 3 bedrooms, a master with BIRs and two way bathroom, separate toilet, brand new kitchen, large laundry with double basins with outdoor access, large family room and formal dining.

The home is unique with the option to use the back of the home as a self-contained unit or a home office as it has a kitchenette area and ensuite, all with its own external access.

Resting in a sought-after neighbourhood, it is close to Sunbury Shopping Centre, Sunbury station, walking distance to Roger Free Park, general stores and pizza shop. Minutes from Calder Freeway, Schools
