120 Woodswallow Entrance, Sunshine North

Modern and Tranquil

Located in a most sought after position of Sunshine North is an almost new 3 bedroom Townhouse that overlooks the Maribyrnong River and its green surrounds.
Designed by the acclaimed architecture firm, Rothelowman, this low-maintenance Townhouse is simple and bold with striking forms and excellent functionality. The living area draws light and sun into the home and the large double sliding doors open to a good-sized deck that overlooks the river. The indulgent master bedroom with ensuite offers sanctuary with large windows filling the room with light and offering more views of the Maribyrnong River.
.
The kitchen is designed with bespoke detailing, Bosch appliances and functionality in mind. It includes vertically routed cabinetry, integrated open shelving and pendant lighting. The quality stone benchtop works well with the white and timber joinery and the matte black tapware.
Relax in your own private garden at the end of a busy day with a paved alfresco area overlooking the Maribyrnong River.
Other features:
- 25 minutes to Melbourne CBD
- Short drive to Sunshine Plaza, Sunshine Station and Highpoint shopping centre
- Minutes to Sunshine North Primary School and Braybrook College
- Easy access to Solomon's Ford and the Maribyrnong River trail
- 15 minutes to Airport
